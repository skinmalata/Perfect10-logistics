Our Story

Over a Decade of Logistics Excellence

Founded in Port Harcourt, Rivers State, Perfect10 Logistics Limited began with a simple mission: to bridge the logistics gap for businesses in Nigeria. What started as a small local haulage service has grown into a comprehensive logistics company serving clients across the nation.

With over 10 years of hands-on experience, we have developed deep expertise in freight management, warehousing, distribution, and supply chain optimization. Our modern fleet, trained personnel, and technology-driven approach ensure that every shipment is handled with precision and care.

We understand the unique challenges of doing business in Nigeria - from infrastructure to regulatory requirements. That's why we've built a robust network that adapts to local conditions while maintaining international standards of service.
