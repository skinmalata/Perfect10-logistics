5 Bedroom Duplex

Castles and Green Estate, Eneka Link Road, Port Harcourt, Rivers State

₦120,000,000

This exquisite 5-bedroom duplex is situated in the prestigious Castles and Green Estate along Eneka Link Road, Port Harcourt. The property boasts premium finishes and sophisticated design throughout.

The ground floor features a grand living room, formal dining area, a state-of-the-art kitchen with premium fittings, guest toilet, and a staff quarters. The upper floor comprises five spacious bedrooms, including a luxurious master suite with his-and-hers closets and a spa-inspired bathroom.

Castles and Green Estate is one of Port Harcourt's most sought-after residential communities, offering 24-hour security, well-lit streets, landscaped common areas, and reliable utilities. Located minutes from shopping centres, schools, and major business districts.
