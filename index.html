What We Offer

Our Core Services

We provide end-to-end logistics solutions designed to streamline your supply chain and keep your business moving forward.

Shipping

Comprehensive ocean freight and container shipping services for imports and exports. We handle FCL, LCL, and breakbulk cargo through major Nigerian ports.

Clearing & Forwarding

Expert customs clearance, documentation, and freight forwarding. We navigate Nigerian Customs regulations to ensure fast, compliant cargo release.

Haulage

Reliable road haulage and trucking across Nigeria. Full truckload and partial load options with GPS-tracked fleet for safe, timely delivery.

Oil & Gas Logistics

Specialized logistics for the oil and gas sector. We handle equipment, pipes, chemicals, and project cargo with industry-specific safety standards.

Air Freight

Fast and secure air cargo services for time-sensitive shipments. Door-to-door delivery from major international airports to any destination in Nigeria.

Bonded Terminal

Licensed bonded terminal operations for customs-bonded storage. Secure warehousing for dutiable cargo with customs supervision and documentation support.

Real Estate

Comprehensive real estate advisory and transaction support. We guide you through property investments with expert market knowledge.

Land Verification

Thorough title searches, survey verification, and due diligence at land registries to confirm authentic, dispute-free property titles.

Property Acquisition

End-to-end property acquisition support including site assessment, negotiation, documentation, and legal processing for seamless transactions.
