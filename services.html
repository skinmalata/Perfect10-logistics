Our Services

What We Do

Comprehensive Logistics Solutions

From freight to final delivery, we handle every step of your supply chain with professionalism and care.

Shipping

We provide comprehensive ocean freight and container shipping services for both import and export operations. Whether you need Full Container Load (FCL), Less than Container Load (LCL), or breakbulk cargo handling, our team coordinates with shipping lines and port authorities to ensure smooth movement of goods through major Nigerian ports including Lagos, Port Harcourt, and Onne.

From booking to delivery, we manage documentation, cargo inspection, and port coordination so you can focus on your core business.

  • FCL & LCL container shipping
  • Breakbulk & project cargo handling
  • Import & export documentation
  • Port-to-port & door-to-door delivery

Clearing & Forwarding

Our licensed clearing and forwarding agents navigate the complexities of Nigerian Customs regulations to ensure your cargo is cleared quickly and compliantly. We handle all documentation including Form M, PAAR, SONCAP, and duty assessments, minimizing delays and demurrage costs at the ports.

With established relationships with Nigerian Customs Service (NCS) and port authorities, we fast-track cargo release and keep you informed at every step.

  • Customs clearance at all Nigerian ports
  • Form M, PAAR, SONCAP processing
  • Import/export documentation & compliance
  • Freight forwarding & cargo tracking

Haulage

Our haulage division provides reliable road transportation across Nigeria with a modern fleet of trucks, trailers, and tankers. We handle container haulage from port to warehouse, bulk cargo movements, and dedicated trucking for industrial and commercial clients.

All vehicles are GPS-tracked, fully insured, and maintained to the highest safety standards, giving you real-time visibility and peace of mind.

  • Container haulage (20ft & 40ft)
  • Flatbed, lowbed & tanker trucks
  • GPS fleet tracking & monitoring
  • Nationwide delivery coverage

Oil & Gas Logistics

We specialize in logistics for the oil and gas sector, handling the movement of drilling equipment, pipes, chemicals, spare parts, and project cargo to onshore and offshore locations. Our team understands the strict safety, environmental, and compliance requirements of the energy industry.

From logistics base operations to rig support and last-mile delivery to remote sites, we keep the energy sector moving.

  • Equipment & pipe logistics
  • Chemical & hazardous cargo handling
  • Rig support & offshore logistics
  • Project cargo & heavy lift

Air Freight

When time is critical, our air freight services provide fast, secure cargo delivery across domestic and international routes. We partner with major airlines and cargo operators to move your shipments from airports to final destination with minimal transit time.

We handle everything from express parcels to large consignments, with door-to-door and airport-to-airport options available.

  • Express & standard air cargo
  • Door-to-door & airport-to-airport
  • Customs clearance included
  • Real-time shipment tracking

Bonded Terminal

Our licensed bonded terminal provides secure, customs-supervised storage for dutiable and imported cargo. Goods can be stored without immediate payment of customs duties, giving you flexibility in managing cash flow and inventory before final clearance.

We maintain full customs documentation and supervision, ensuring compliance with Nigerian Customs bonded warehouse regulations.

  • Customs-bonded storage facility
  • Duty-deferred cargo handling
  • 24/7 security & monitoring
  • Full customs documentation support
